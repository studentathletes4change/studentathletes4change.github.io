OUR MISSION

Empowering Student-Athletes to Enforce Change

This organization was formed in response to the murders of George Floyd, Breonna Taylor, Ahmaud Arbery, and countless others. Racism, sexism, and other forms of discrimination are extremely prevalent in collegiate athletics and are constantly swept under the rug in favor of the oppressor. Student-Athletes 4 Change was formed to create a safe platform for student-athletes to share their personal experiences with these issues in athletics. Our organization wants to help implement widespread change within teams, departments, and the NCAA holistically.
